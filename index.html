CALLISTO

is the third largest moon of Jupiter and has the oldest unchanged surface in the solar system. Callisto is the largest moon without any geological activity.

IO

is the fourth-largest moon of Jupiter and has the most volcanic activity in the solar system. Io is covered completely in volcanoes with frequent eruptions.

EUROPA

is a moon of Jupiter and has the smoothest surface in the solar system. Due to the tidal heating from Jupiter there is an ocean that exists under its icy crust.

TRITON

is the largest moon of Neptune and the only large moon with a retrograde motion. Triton is volcanic active moon erupting liquid water and ammonia.

ENCELADUS

is the sixth-largest moon of Saturn and the most reflective moon in the solar system. Enceladus is the source of Saturn’s E-ring is created by its geyser.

MIRANDA

is a moon of Uranus and is the ugliest moon in our solar system. Miranda has some of the greatest varied landscapes.

NEREID

is the third largest moon of Neptune and has the most eccentric orbit in our solar system.

MIMAS

is a moon of Saturn and is the smallest moon that is round shaped due to self-gravitation. Mimas resemblance the Death Star with its large crater.
